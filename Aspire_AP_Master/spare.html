Our Values

Providing Support:

We are committed to provide the wrap around support that young people and their families need to enable pupils to overcome barriers beyond the school that may be hindering their education.

Building Resilience:

We are committed to equipping students with the principles and life skills that will enable them to progress despite the challenges they face in life in general.

Creating Opportunity:

We are committed to ensuring the school is a place where pupils are exposed to a range of opportunities including games, cooking, outdoor activities, day trips and even overseas travel to majority world contexts..

Enabling Achievement:

We are committed to ensure pupils progress from their base line assessment, particularly in core subjects. That they are well prepared to succeed in examinations and move on seamlessly to their next chapter in life, whether that be education, training or employment.
